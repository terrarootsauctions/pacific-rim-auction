How Pacific Rim Auction Works

Transparent. Fast. Trusted.

🏠

1. List or Browse

Sellers submit properties with verified titles. Buyers browse live auctions globally with full transparency.

🔍

2. Due Diligence

Review inspection reports, title documents, and AI valuations. Our licensed agent network is at your service.

🔨

3. Bid with Confidence

Place bids in real-time. Our 7-Day Buyer Confidence Guarantee lets you verify before you commit.

🏘️

4. Close via Terra Roots

Physical properties move to Terra Roots Global Realty Solutions LLC for sale and closing. All disclosures delivered. Fees deposited directly to account.
