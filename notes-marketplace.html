How the Notes Marketplace Works

Transparent. Verified. Global.

📋

1. Note Submission

Note holders submit instruments with full documentation: original promissory note, deed of trust/mortgage, payment history, title report.

🔍

2. Due Diligence Package

Every note includes a complete due diligence package. Review lien position, UPB, payment history, and collateral property value.

🌞

3. Follow-the-Sun Auction

Auctions open market-by-market as the sun moves. Hawaii opens first, then US West Coast, London, Dubai, Singapore — maximum global exposure.

📝

4. Assignment & Close

Winning bidder receives note assignment within 14 days. Blockchain-verified transfer. Full legal documentation provided by jurisdiction.
